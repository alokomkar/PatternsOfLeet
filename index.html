Too many goals. Too much information. Not enough clarity.

Modern professionals have access to AI assistants, productivity apps, calendars and notes. Yet long term goals still become fragmented. Intent exists to help people maintain continuity and decide what deserves attention today.

Built around meaningful goals.

Intent is not a note taking app or a task manager. Everything in Intent exists in the context of a goal.

🎯 Goal-first planning

Create meaningful goals across career, business, education, finance, health or personal growth.
